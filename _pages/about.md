## About Me

I'm a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at [Lehigh University](https://www2.lehigh.edu/). My research focuses on Deep Learning (Generative Modeling, Contrastive Learning, Cross-Modal Attention), Multimodal Learning, Medical Image Computing, and Brain Imaging Genomics.

I am very fortunate to be advised by [Prof. Lifang He](https://engineering.lehigh.edu/faculty/lifang-he), [Prof. Yu Zhang](https://profiles.stanford.edu/176276), and [Prof. Brian Y. Chen](https://www.cse.lehigh.edu/~chen/).

## Research Interests

* **Medical AI**: Medical multimodal learning, generative modeling, and interpretable neural networks for disease prediction and clinical decision support

* **Knowledge Graphs and LLMs**: Knowledge graph completion, semantic relation extraction, and integration with large language models for intelligent reasoning in educational and biomedical domains

* **Multimodal Data Integration**: Fusion and representation learning across imaging, text, and genomics, with applications in diagnosis, biomarker discovery, and personalized recommendation
